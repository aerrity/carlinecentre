Governance and Funding

The Board of Management oversees policy formation, strategic planning, recruitment, finance and evaluation.

Currently, the Board consists of a legal professional, finance professional, retired business professionals, retired teachers Dublin City Council members, members of the Order of Saint Francis, and members of the public.

The Board are voluntary and receive no remuneration for their work. The Centre Manager attends Board meetings. Subcommitees of the Board meet monthly and are as follows

  • Education
  • Maintenance
  • Finance
  • Land Bank
