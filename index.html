Welcome to the Carline Learning Centre

What we do

Situated in Lucan, County Dublin, we are a Learning Centre for young people aged 13 - 17 years old, excluded from or no longer attending mainstream education.

There are up to 30 young people children in our Centre in classes from First Year to Junior Certificate.

We currently have 14 staff - a Manager, Social Care staff, teaching staff and a counsellor. Support staff includes an administrator secretary, cook, bus driver and cleaning staff.

Carline Centre Facilities

The Centre has three dedicated classrooms and a custom-built wood workshop. There is also a fully equipped IT room with Internet access. We have an art room, a hair and beauty room, a dining room, kitchen, a psychology/counselling room.

We also have a large communal area where the young people eat, run activities, play pool and do group work. In the garden we have a polytunnel and vegetable patches for growing items for the kitchen. Our music room is in its final stages of completion.

The Centre also has a minibus to collect the young people, attend sports and activities.
